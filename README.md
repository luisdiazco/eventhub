## About The Project

Event Map App
Event Map is a powerful event management app that allows users to discover events, buy tickets and share their experiences. With a focus on simplicity, the app provides an intuitive map-based user interface that makes it easy to find and attend events in your area.

Features
* Discover events near you: Browse a dynamic map of events happening in your area, sorted by popularity, date and genre.
* Purchase tickets: Buy tickets for your favorite events with just a few taps.
* Share your experience: Share your photos, reviews, and ratings of events with the community to help others make informed decisions.
* Personalized recommendations: Receive personalized recommendations based on your interests and the events you have attended.
* Secure ticket management: Manage your tickets securely and access them at any time from the app or through email.
